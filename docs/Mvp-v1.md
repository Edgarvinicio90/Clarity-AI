# Clarity AI MVP v1

## Goal

Help a business owner understand the health of their company in less than 5 minutes.

## Inputs

- Revenue
- Expenses
- Cash balance
- Debt
- Owner hours worked
- Number of employees

## Outputs

### Business Health Score

Score from 0–100 based on:
- Profitability
- Cash position
- Growth
- Debt
- Efficiency

### Key Insights

Top 3 strengths.

Top 3 risks.

### Recommended Action

One action that will have the highest impact on the business.

## Success Metric

A business owner can answer:

"Is my business healthy?"

without needing accounting knowledge.
