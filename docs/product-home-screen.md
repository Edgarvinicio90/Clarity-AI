# First 3 Things Carlos Should See

## 1. Today’s Business Result

Carlos should immediately see:

- How much money came in
- How much money went out
- Estimated profit
- Cash available

Simple question answered:

> Did I make money today?

---

## 2. Missed Opportunities

Clarity AI should explain why the business could have made more money.

Examples:

- Best-selling product ran out
- Labor cost was too high
- Certain menu items had low margin
- Sales were strong but profit was weak
- A promotion did not generate enough return

Simple question answered:

> Where did I lose money or miss profit?

---

## 3. Tomorrow’s Action Plan

Carlos should see what to prepare for the next day.

Examples:

- How much cash to keep available
- Which bills are coming soon
- What products to restock
- Whether staffing should be adjusted
- One recommended action to improve tomorrow

Simple question answered:

> What should I do tomorrow to protect my business?
